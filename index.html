Research Interests

Distributed ML Systems

Designing fault-tolerant training and inference pipelines that scale to thousands of nodes with minimal communication overhead.

SystemsDistributedMLSys

Algorithmic Fairness

Formal guarantees for equitable decision-making in large language models and recommender systems, with provable bounds.

FairnessTheoryNLP

FairDecode Toolkit
